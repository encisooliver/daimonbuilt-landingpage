Diamondbuilt Development Corporation



About Us

Diamondbuilt Development Corporation is a real estate company based in Cebu City and was founded by a businesswoman who is a business graduate at the University of San Carlos. The name Diamondbuilt is inspired by her perspective of the company, it means unbeatable, long-lasting, classy and a symbol of success.

Diamondbuilt Development Corporation is established in 2018 and continues to operate based on the principle of providing high quality commercial and residential properties to individuals, families, and investors. DBDC strongly believes in upholding exceptional service to the customers from their reservation to finally having their dream place they wish to keep for life.

Mission

Our projects at Diamondbuilt Development Corporation is to develop and deliver a comfortable living environment across the entire nation and become one of the most reputable and valuable real estate developers in the country with integrity and accountability using both proven and innovative methods.

Vision

Our vision is to deliver a high quality Residential and Commercial projects that correlate with Global developers.
We intend to achieve this vision in the context of our core values.

  • Efficiency
  • Growth
  • Professionalism
  • Quality

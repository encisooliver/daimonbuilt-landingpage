COMPANY PROFILE

Diamondbuilt Development Corporation is a newly established company that is dedicated to provide individuals, families, and investors with high quality commercial and residential properties. We strongly believe in upholding exceptional service to our clients.
Diamondbuilt was founded by Ms. Ivy Jocel N. Balsa in 2018. Imperial Heights is our flagship subdivision with 122 units and commercial spaces located in Binaliw, Cebu City and soon will be one of the most sophisticated and safest subdivisions in Cebu.

Mission

Company Background

ADiamondBuilt Development Corporation is a developer based in Cebu City with a vision to deliver quality homes to every family and individual. We develop land owned by Sps. Carlo Villamor at Binaliw, Cebu City. The develop property will be named Imperial Heights Subdivision and soon will be one of the most sophisticated and safest subdivision in Cebu. There will be one hundred twenty-two houses that will be built in ten thousand square meters with a view of the Queen City of the South.
